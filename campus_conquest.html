Final Exam: Campus Conquest

Date: Spring 2024

Overview

Final Exam: Campus Conquest was our Spring 2024 project, inviting guests to participate in a life-sized board game experience. Players competed in teams, solving puzzles across four themed zones—Adventure, Alchemy, Library, and Botany—blending escape room challenges with strategic gameplay.

Story

Guests are invited by the eccentric Professor Konn to complete a unique exam: playing a modified version of Risk. To pass, they must solve puzzles and expand their territory faster than opposing teams. Each themed zone features puzzles inspired by escape rooms and scavenger hunts, requiring teamwork, strategy, and problem-solving to emerge victorious.

My Role

- Assisted in set dressing and final touches to ensure the room was presentable

- Created a banner and other decortions

- Game Guided and helped run the project for guests

Skills Used

Painting, structure building, teamwork, event coordination, detail-oriented
