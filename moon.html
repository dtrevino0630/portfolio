Missing on the Moon

Date: Fall 2023

Overview

TxTPED’s Fall 2023 escape room. Over 100 guests experienced 3 immersive rooms and solved 11 interconnected puzzles.

Story

Guests are tasked with rescuing missing astronauts stranded on a moon base. Along the way, they must avoid a lurking alien lifeform, restore power, and regulate air pressure to ensure a safe escape.

My Role

  • Designed and prototyped interactive puzzles
  • Collaborated on set design and room layout
  • Integrated technical props with Arduino and electronics

Skills Used

Mechatronics, teamwork, puzzle design, Arduino prototyping
