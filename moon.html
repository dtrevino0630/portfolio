Missing on the Moon

Date: Fall 2023

Overview

Missing on the Moon was our Fall 2023 escape room, welcoming over 100 guests from November 1–4. The experience featured three interconnected rooms and 11 unique puzzles, combining teamwork, problem-solving, and hands-on interaction for an immersive adventure.

Story

Guests are recruited to rescue a group of astronauts who have gone missing from a moon base. After teleporting to the base and exploring the living quarters, they gather clues to unlock each astronaut’s locker and discover passwords. Upon completing the puzzle sequence, the captain's audio log reveals that the previous astronauts were attacked by an alien life form. Guests must then restore power, fix the air pressure, and escape the moon base before the aliens return.

My Role

- Designed and prototyped structure and doors

- Collaborated on set design and room layout

Skills Used

Structural design, teamwork, feasibility, communication, creative-thinking
